Welcome

We absolutely adore the novels of Thomas Hardy written and set in England in the late 19th century.
We want to bring Hardy's stories to life and make them more accessible to modern audiences.

Behind the scenes

We are a grassroots community project led by a local young Dorset film maker, Rose, who is working voluntarily with other amateurs to make one Hardy feature film and a couple of dramatic podcasts per year.

The Wessex Dramas Project shoots in Dorset, primarily at weekends, and welcomes everyone who wants to play as either cast, crew, or production team.

We exhibit our films as public showings for free (unless as a charity fundraiser) with a free Q&A session from the director: only expenses are asked for. We podcast our audio plays on this web site and other platforms.

The project enters its films at international film festivals. It is is also not averse to approach from sponsors!
